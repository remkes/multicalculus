In \(\RR^3\text{,}\) consider the three-dimensional spiral \(\gamma(t) = (\cos t, \sin t, t)\text{.}\) On the domain \(t \in [0, 8\pi]\text{,}\) the spiral makes four revolutions. I calculate its arclength.
\begin{equation*} L = \int_0^{8\pi} \sqrt{ \sin^2 t + \cos^2 t + 1} dt = \int_0^{8\pi} \sqrt{2} dt = 8 \sqrt{2} \pi \end{equation*}
