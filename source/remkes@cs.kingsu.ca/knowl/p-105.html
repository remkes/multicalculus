In three dimensions, in addition to spiraling outward or inward, a curve can spiral upward or downward. The curve \(\gamma(t) = (t \cos t, t \sin t, t)\text{,}\) for \(t \in [0,\infty)\text{,}\) is a conical spiral extending infinitely upward.
