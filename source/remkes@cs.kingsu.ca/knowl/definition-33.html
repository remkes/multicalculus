Definition 3.1.7.

Let \(A \subset \RR^n\) be the domain of a scalar field \(f: A \rightarrow \RR\text{.}\) The graph of the scalar field is the subset of \(\RR^{n+1}\) consisting of all points \((x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n, f(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n))\) for \((x_1, x_2, \ldots x_n) \in A\text{.}\)
