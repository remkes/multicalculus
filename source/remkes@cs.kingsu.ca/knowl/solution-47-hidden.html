I calculate the two partial derivative.
\begin{gather*} \frac{\del}{\del x} f(x,y) = -8x \\ \frac{\del}{\del y} f(x,y) = 3 \end{gather*}
The gradient has these two as components.
\begin{equation*} \nabla f(x,y) = (-8x,3) \end{equation*}
