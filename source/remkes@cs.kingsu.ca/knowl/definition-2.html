Definition 1.1.2.

A vector is a finite ordered list of scalars. Vectors can be written either as columns or rows. If a vector is a list of the numbers \(4\text{,}\) \(15\text{,}\) \(\pi\) and \(-e\) (in that order), I write the vector in one of two ways:
\begin{align*} \amp \begin{pmatrix} 4 \\ 15 \\ \pi \\ -e \end{pmatrix} \amp \text{or} \amp \amp (4, 15, \pi, -e) \amp \end{align*}
