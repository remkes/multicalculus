  1. \(\displaystyle t = 3s\)
  2. \(\displaystyle t = \frac{s}{4}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle t = s + 7\)
  4. \(\displaystyle t = s - 10\)
  5. \(\displaystyle t = s^2\)
  6. \(\displaystyle t = \sqrt{s}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle t = e^s\)
  8. \(\displaystyle t = -s\)
