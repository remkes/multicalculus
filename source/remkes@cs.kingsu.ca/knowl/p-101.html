Notice that this parametric curve is the graph of a function, specifically the function \(f(x) = \frac{1}{x}\) between \(x = \frac{1}{5}\) and \(x=5\text{.}\) Parametric curves where one of the form \(\gamma(t) = (t,f(t))\) just trace the graph of the function \(f\) over some parts of it domain.
