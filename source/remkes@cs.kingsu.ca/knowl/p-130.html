For each straight line segment, the length of the segment is given by using pythagorus on the triange formed by the segment and the changes in each variable. I know \(\Delta y\) and \(\Delta x\text{,}\) then the length of the segment is \(\sqrt{\Delta x^2 + \Delta y^2}\text{.}\) I approximate the total length of the curve by adding up the lengths of these segments.
\begin{equation*} L \cong \sum \sqrt{\Delta x^2 + \Delta y^2} \end{equation*}
