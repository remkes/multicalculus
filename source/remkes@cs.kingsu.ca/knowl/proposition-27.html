Proposition 10.2.8.

Let \(F\) be a vector field on \(U\) an open simply-connected set in \(\RR^n\) and let \(\gamma: [a,b] \rightarrow U\) be a parametric curve. The following four conditions are equivalent.
  1. \(F\) is conservative on \(U\text{.}\) (By definition, this means \(F = \nabla f\) for a scalar field \(f\)).
  2. The line integrals of \(F\) are path independent in \(U\text{.}\)
  3. The line integral of \(F\) is zero for any closed path in \(U\text{.}\)
  4. \(\nabla \times F = 0\text{.}\)
